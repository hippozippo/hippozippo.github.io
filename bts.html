Beyond The Scope

Do you possess the power of changing the world with your story? Then let your camera reels role and show us what you got. We bring to you BTS- A short movie making competition

Prize Money: 3000 INR
Last Date : 13th January 2015

Guidelines for short film making:
1) Maximum movie length 10 mins.
2) 1 min trailer
3) No specific theme.
4) Entries can be in any language. However, they must include english subtitles.
5) Original background music if needed.
6) Video with the maximum number of likes gets a big heads-up.
7) The movie clip should not include any previously created work, like an already existing movie directly being copy-pasted in the entry. All works must be original. Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
8) The short film should not contain any sense of vulgarity. Use of abusive words, nudity, lewd or otherwise offensive/inappropriate content is banned. The entries must not contain any sensitive matter that might hurt the feelings of a culture/language/religion/region.
9) Already existing movie clips can be edited via video editing softwares and then put into the submission entries, but direct copy-pasting will result in disqualification. No copyright protected material to be included.
10) The submission deadline and time limit criteria to be taken serious care of by the participants.
11) The main focus for judging a particular ad clip would be the concept and execution.
12) The movie may include text, images, photos, videos or any combination of these, of a clear and good resolution.

Negative marking for the disobedience of any of the above-mentioned rules. The short films shall be judged based on content, expressions, fluency in narration(if any) and story.
