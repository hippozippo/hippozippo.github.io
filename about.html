The Literary Circle


Literary Circle, NIT Durgapur is an apex body in literary activities. Something which is infinitely more important than that is the simple fact that we try to achieve the zenith in every endeavour that we undertake. There are great many things which are demanded from our own composition and we try to live up to our standing as best as we can. Our leadership skills are fabled in the countless fests and events that we have anchored since our inception.

Verve, the literary-youth fest: which is held every odd semester, has developed a niche of its own among the college populace and beyond.

NITMUN, Model United Nations: NITMUN with its three successful sessions continues to promote world peace and harmony allowing students to engage in problem solving, learn aspects of conflict resolution, research and communication skills, and creates the opportunity to meet new people and make new friends all over the country.

Déjà vu, the official college newsletter: Published once every semester, this is the official college newsletter, highlighting all the important activities and events in this esteemed Institution.

Yearbook: The compilation of memoirs and memories for all pass-outs, containing details of all students of The Batch, so that they can never forget their Alma Mater.

The pride and joy of the college - a role that we have made our own has come through much perseverance. It is our vision that we never lose sight of our past while marching forward. Our goal is to never sit on our laurels and make blinding progress in our field to which we owe to our depth of character.
